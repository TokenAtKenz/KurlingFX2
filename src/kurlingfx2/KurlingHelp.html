How To Play Krazy Kurling

Press NEW GAME, the button will change to SHOOT
Press SHOOT after you have set up the shot.
After all the rocks are shot, the button will change
to SCORE END. Press SCORE END to update the scoreboard
The button then changes to NEXT END.
Press NEXT END to reset and start the next end.

the game keeps track of scoring, hammer and ends.

pretty much, click the one button to play. NEW GAME

There are 4 basic game controls.

Rock Positioning
Broom Positioning
Speed of the Rock
Speed and Direction of the Curl


using your mouse, the ROCK can be moved left or right.

The ROCK is always aimed towards the center of the BROOM.
The BROOM can be positioned anywhere on or off the ice.

The ROCKSPEED is set by the scrollbar on the left side of
of the Ice Surface. The speed goes from zero to max.

The Curl SPEED and DIRECTION, are set with the scrollbar directly
above the Ice Surface. The rock will curl in the direction of the
arrow, and the rotation speed will be zero to max in whatever direction is chosen.

There are no markings on the speed or curl bar, just as there
are no markings on the ice or the rocks to set the speed and curl.
A big part of the game is getting a "feel" for it.
There are other buttons:

Turn Curl OFF/ON : toggles the curl scrollbar. when off, all shots are straight

Put Sides UP/DOWN : toggles the side walls between bounce-back AND out-off-bounds

Put Ends UP/DOWN : toggles toe front and back walls.

Watch Demo : this will trigger an automated end of curling. Very bad curling.
the demo will be created using the existing wall setup, and
is rendered as a single new and unique animated sequence.

RESET : does a reset. Work in progress as to exactly what ends up being reset
